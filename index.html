Computational Imaging and Data Processing

High-speed recordings of neural activity are fundamentally contaminated by out-of-focus fluorescense and stochastic noise. Here, I showcase computational imaging techniques that use structured light and algorithmic image reconstruction to increase the SNR of recorded data.

A. Optical sectioning structured illumination microscopy (OS-SIM) with self-supervised deep learning (SSL) for denoising.
